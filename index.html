(A Strange But Beautiful Occurence)

Here I see the sun is seemingly
teaming with beams
churning the furnace
burning like ancient kings
and Saturn never had more rings
than the queen's fingers -
but the sun lingers
celestial singers are never reached
by Grecian wings.
